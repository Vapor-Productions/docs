## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Premier Bot ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses,
and protects information when you use our services and documentation hosted at
`https://premierplanner.eu/docs/legal/privacy-policy`.

## Information we collect

- Account data: usernames, Discord IDs, and team identifiers.
- Usage data: commands used, timestamps, and feature interactions.
- Device data: basic browser or client metadata for troubleshooting.

## How we use information

- Provide and improve Premier Bot features and reliability.
- Manage rosters, schedules, and matchday workflows.
- Respond to support requests and security issues.

## Data retention

We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by
law. You can request deletion at any time.

## Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with service providers
for hosting, analytics, or support, under confidentiality obligations.

## Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect data.
No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete
your personal information. Contact us to exercise these rights.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted
at `https://premierplanner.eu/docs/legal/privacy-policy`.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at [support@premierplanner.eu](mailto:support@premierplanner.eu).
